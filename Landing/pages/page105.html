ENVIRONMENT- ROLE OF STUDENT

We are directly or indirectly dependent on the environment. We gained all the resources or components which are required for the survival of living things from the environment. The basic necessities of the life that are air, water, soil, food, shelter, etc. are obtained through the environment. Not only human beings but also plants and animals are depended on the environment.

We live in a time when the environment is in danger. We need a healthy environment in order to survive. Environment protection is the need of the hour. It helps to prevent health problems, to maintain the ecosystem, and to preserve the Earth for the next generation. Ecosystem is very important as Plant life, animal life, and people all depend on each other. An unhealthy environment disturbs this ecosystem. For example, changes in the environments might cause a certain kind of plant to die. If that plant is food for a certain kind of animal, the animal will die too. If people use that animal as food source, there could be big problem. Further, without clean air to breathe, clean water to drink, a healthy ecosystem, the human race will not survive.
